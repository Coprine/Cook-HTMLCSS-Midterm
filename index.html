Mustard

It's yellow, it hurts to eat, nobody likes it, not even me, but here it is, it's mustard, really it needs no introduction, just look at it!

I think mustard was Greek, but modern mustard was invented in mid-century 1900's.

That red stuff that looks like mustard

Always getting these things confused with mustard, has led to more than one occasion where I was surpised when I took a drink.

The stuff they used to use to make mustard

They don't use as much now, although technically they do, basically mustard used to really hurt to drink but I did it anyway.

As Greeks invented mustard, naturally they invented this thing, too.

Extra chunky green mustard

You can't even drink this one, you have to eat it, downright disgusting, not even in my top 3 mustards, gross, ew.

Originated in India, not even real mustard, isn't even Greek.

Ice, I guess
